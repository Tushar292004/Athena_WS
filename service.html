Our Services

  • Live Tracking

    The app will continuously track the user's location and map the path they follow. The live tracking feature will also allow the user's guardians and trusted persons to track them in all cases.

  • EMERGENCY SHARED FILES

    The app will include a photo and video sharing feature that will enable users to share photos, videos, and audio if they feel threatened by someone before engaging with them to there trusted person's account.

  • SAFER ROUTES

    The application will also suggest safer routes to the user to eliminate the risk of accidents. The app will alert users if the path goes nearby bars and liquor stores or quiet and unnoticeable places where accidents can take place. The app will suggest an alternative path if possible.

  • SOS RINGER

    The user can press the power button three times to initiate an SOS alert and the app will send an audio recording , images, videos of the last few minutes and the location where the SOS was initiated, to the trusted persons and nearby police station.

  • ACCOUNT MANAGMENT

    Change your detials , address , email , mobile number , photo , etc.
